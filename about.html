ABOUT ME

I help people and companies find answers and solve business problems with Data.

I have undaunted interest in the big data industry and have been challenging myself in exploratory data analysis, model creation and testing, and visualizations. I have cognate experience in handling financial, business, operational, and social data, as well as the banking industry itineraries.

Currently, I am a Data Scientist at Accion Microfinance Bank Limited, Nigeria. I am tasked with the responsibilities of creating, updating, managing and analyzing the entire bank's transactional and Analytical data. Creating ML models to support business. I am involved in the CBN's anti money laundering project as Data Scientist and handles data integrity, quality and reliability part of the project.

I conduct academic and industrial research with specialty in the analytical aspect while I am a voracious reader and have penchant in theoretical writing. Some of my publications can be accessed via https://www.medium.com/@chidiebere.vincent

I am versatile in the use of analytics tools such as Power BI, Excel, and Tableau. Query tools such SQL Server and Postgresql, and programming languages such as Python and R.

I have a BSc (Honours) in Geography from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka. I specialised in Remote Sensing and GIS Analysis with a research in the analysis of Land Use Land Cover Detection with the use of Quantum GIS tools.

With my knowledge and interest in big data, I am currently undergoing trainings on Data Engineering to have a first hand experience of building data pipelines and harnessing data from disparate sources and utilizing my current analytical skills for impacting contributions in different industries.
